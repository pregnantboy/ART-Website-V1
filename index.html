Insurance for the Masses

Insurance for the Masses is insurance that thrives at low premium levels. IFM products are powered by state-of-the-art risk quantification methods, GIS tools and cloud computing technologies. These products provide viable, effective and sustainable risk protection to developing communities.

Here’s how it works

Key Features

Anyone, Anywhere

We adopt the ‘Insurance in a Card’ concept to simplify processes and make IFM products accessible and available to many developing communities. Customers can purchase Insurance Cards off the shelves and activate their insurance coverage easily via a network of IFM card machines.

Location Based Payouts

Immediately after a catastrophe, our proprietary software will flag out affected accounts and determine the payout amounts based on known event parameters.

Funds when you need them

Triggered payouts are automatically credited into Insurance Cards of affected customers, which customers can utilize to collect their payouts at accessible locations nationwide.

Beneficiaries

People

IFM provides effective risk protection for the masses. IFM products are easy to understand, simple to use, and accessible to all. IFM offers a much-needed safety net for developing communities, protecting their productive assets and improving their livelihoods.

Insurers

IFM brings mass markets opportunities for insurance companies via new product offerings. This new breed of insurance products will significantly lower administrative expenses via simplified and automated processes, and make the low-premium business work for the insurers’ portfolios.

Government

Voluntary participation of the masses in IFM make such insurance schemes sustainable in the long run, without taking a toll on the state’s coffers. Insurance help lessen the financial burden of governments during catastrophic events as huge losses are being transferred out to global financial markets.
