How It Works

Browse and Select

Browse and compare IFM policies from partnering insurance companies via the ARTS mobile application. Depending on the policy type, you will input only the essential information (zipcode, home construction type, etc.) with your desired policy coverage limit.

Dynamic Pricing

Based on the user information and policy limits provided, our proprietary algorithms will determine the appropriate premium amounts that you should pay. We will also create the even payout table that shows how much you stand to receive during a catastrophe (dependent on even severity).

Purchase and Activate

Now that you are well aware of the premium amounts and payout conditions, you can proceed to make premium payments via electronic or physical means. A confirmation receipt will be issued to confirm the terms and validity of your IFM policy.

Payout Calculation

When a disaster strikes, our system will retrieve post-event event parameters and information from independent and verifiable sources. The ARTS software will process this information and determine payout amounts for each and every user account surrounding the disaster region.

Fund Collection

If you are a registered policyholder within the payout zones, you will receive a notification that the payouts have been credited into your user account. You can choose to receive your payout amounts electronically or physically at accessible location nationwide.
