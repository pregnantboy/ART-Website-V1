Technology-based Solutions

Risk Quantification

We help insurers, government bodies, and related agencies create IFM products through our proprietary risk software. Our systems and algorithms provide the pricing, payout and risk statistics needed to write the IFM business.

Distribution Infrastructure

We provide the cyber-physical tools needed to package and distribute insurance across various platforms and channels, including insurance cards, mobile apps, etc. Our distribution platform transfers underwriting data on products sold everywhere into our servers automatically.

Portfolio Management

Our cloud management system is a one-stop portal for our partner institutions to manage their IFM business. Whether in Beijing, Delhi, or Jakarta, our partners can access customer databases and run analyses on their portfolio.

We provide integrated data visualization and analytics tools, enabling our partners to write better business.

When a catastrophe occurs, our algorithms will automatically determine the appropriate payouts for every single policy sold based on actual event statistics from an independent and verifiable source.
